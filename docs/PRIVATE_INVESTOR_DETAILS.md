## Key Financial Metrics

### Unit Economics

**Cost Per Market Created**:
- Gas cost (Base L2): $0.50-2
- Platform overhead: ~$5 (infrastructure, support)
- **Total cost**: $5.50-7 per market

**Revenue Per Market**:
- Average market volume: $10K-50K
- Platform fees (1.2%): $120-600
- Creator fees (0.9% operator share): $90-450
- **Total revenue per market**: $210-1,050

**Gross Margin Per Market**: 95-99% (after subtracting $5.50-7 cost)

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Trader Acquisition**:
- Organic (creator-driven): $0 (creators bring their audience)
- Paid acquisition: $20-50 per user
- Blended CAC: $5-15 per user (mostly organic)

**Creator Acquisition**:
- Top-tier influencers: $1,000-5,000 (guaranteed minimums, onboarding)
- Mid-tier creators: $200-500 (promotional support)
- Long-tail creators: $0 (self-service, no acquisition cost)
- **Blended CAC**: $100-300 per creator

### Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Trader LTV** (3-year horizon):
- Average monthly volume per user: $2,000
- Average fee per user: $42/month (2.1% on losing 50%)
- Retention rate: 30% after 12 months
- **LTV**: $504 (12 months × $42)

**LTV:CAC Ratio**: 50:1 ($504 LTV ÷ $10 CAC)

**Creator LTV** (3-year horizon):
- Average monthly volume per creator: $25K
- Platform operator share: $225/month
- Retention rate: 60% after 12 months
- **LTV**: $2,700 (12 months × $225)

**LTV:CAC Ratio**: 13.5:1 ($2,700 LTV ÷ $200 CAC)

### Payback Period

**Trader Payback**: 0.5 months ($10 CAC ÷ $42 monthly revenue)
**Creator Payback**: 0.9 months ($200 CAC ÷ $225 monthly revenue)

**Conclusion**: Extremely efficient unit economics, mostly organic growth
